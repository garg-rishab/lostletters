Used to Love You

It was all in vain! Wasn’t it? The Poems, The Letters; I wrote you The Love, The Care; I adored you I Love you & I believed you did too… That was my fault; They used to tell me, that you don’t deserve me; Here I was, ready to throw away everything for you… I used to Love you! - Rishabh
